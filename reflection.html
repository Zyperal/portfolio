Class Reflection

A look back at the graphic design program — what was learned, what was built, and where it's all headed.

// key takeaway

"No one will be the helmsman of my luge byt myself!!! No one shall live my life in my stead!!!"

What did you learn this year?
Growth

The Pen Tool really clicked with me easily but to be deathly honest, most of the assignments in this class were fundamental to building and rounding out my skillset as an aspiring graphic artist.

Which project are you most proud of and why?
Favorite

Movie poster. For the sheer fact that I got to make a movie poster off of one of my heists from Payday 2: the Golden Grin Casino

Reflect on Your Strengths
Introspective

My Strengths for the most part were the attention to datails and the collective culmination of ideas that are moshed with every project I do.

What was the hardest part of the program?
Challenge

Time management and HEAVY PROCRASINATION, due to how funky my brain is when deciding what to prioritize, I tend to so work and bounce around to other things and when the deadline comes around I just rush the job and botch it in the process.

Least Fravorite Project?
Old Man Diatribe

Self-Portrait. didn't like the idea at 1st and in my personal opinion, the most botched and sloppy of my works shown.

What project would i redo if i could?
Retrospective

Lanndscape, had i had a bit more time and energy i would've been close to creating Big Sur solely using the Pen Tool and the Shapes in Illustrator.
