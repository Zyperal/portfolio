Casino Crashers
Movie poster · Adobe Photoshop

Movie poster for a fictional heist film. An overhead bird's-eye view of a craps table anchors the composition, with layered prop elements — money bags, a crowbar, a skull mask — scattered around the edge. The typographic treatment follows real theatrical poster conventions including actor billing, studio logos, and a rating block.

Design decisions
Overhead bird's-eye composition
3-tier typographic hierarchy
Red, white & yellow palette
Studio billing block
MPAA rating treatment
